Today's paper of the day is Infrastructuring the Digital Public Sphere by Julie E. Cohen. This paper examines the role of digital tracking and advertising infrastructures in content governance on digital platforms. It interrogates assumptions about public communication and questions the focus on ownership and control over other aspects of systemic configuration. It uses the idea of a "public sphere" as an ideal for comparison of the imperfect reality.
