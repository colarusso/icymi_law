The team had been responsible for translating policy into product development and ensuring AI features met ethical standards. Microsoft has since stated that its Office of Responsible AI will continue to provide cross-company support for reviewing sensitive use cases and advocating for policies that protect customers. The news comes as Microsoft continues to quickly expand its AI-driven products.
