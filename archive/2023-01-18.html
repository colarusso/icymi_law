The report focused almost exclusively on Trump’s actions leading up to the riot, missing an opportunity to hold social media companies accountable.

This podcast episode features Laurent Richard and Sandrine Rigaud, the authors of Pegasus: How a Spy in Your Pocket Threatens the End of Privacy, Dignity, and Democracy. They discuss the investigative effort led by 17 international media organizations which exposed how some governments use the Pegasus spyware against journalists, human rights activists, political dissidents, and others. They talk about the operational security concerns, how they coordinated the international effort, and the impact of their work.
